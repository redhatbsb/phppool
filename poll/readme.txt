Installation:
1. Download and extract Simple PHP Poll
   and upload it to your webserver.
2. CHMOD all files to 777 (Linux only)
3. Navigate to
   http://www.yoursite.com/vote/setup.php
   in your web browser.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions.
5. Delete setup.php and /docs/, and
   CHMOD all files to 666 except for
   /denied/ which should be 644 (Linux Only)
6. Embed poll.php onto whatever web pages
   that you want.
